01 — The Problem

Every year, lakhs of farmers lose their crop to no buyer, no information.

  • ✕ Which nearby buyer is paying the best price
  • ✕ Whether cold storage is available nearby
  • ✕ Real-time mandi (market) rates
  • ✕ Transport options to reach the buyer in time

Small and marginal farmers grow the crop but have almost no visibility into where to sell it, at what price, or how to move it before it spoils. Middlemen exploit the gap — buying cheap and reselling at multiples in the city. The farmer who did the actual work earns the least.

30–40%of Indian farm produce is wasted before it reaches market.
₹Croresin farmer income lost every year to this information gap.
#1cause: crop loss is a leading driver of farmer financial distress.
02 — The Answer

"Yeh buyer paas mein hai, yeh price mil raha hai, yeh cold storage khali hai" — if every farmer could see this on a simple app, or hear it over SMS and a voice call in their own language, most of this loss disappears.

03 — Why Kisan Setu

Three things no one else in the region is doing together.

01

Real-time buyers & prices, nearby

Farmers see who's buying and at what price in their own block — not a city-wide average that hides the real deal on offer.

02

Cold storage before the crop spoils

Live storage availability is matched the moment a harvest is listed, so produce doesn't rot while a buyer is being found.

03

SMS & voice — no smartphone required

A helpline call or a text message does everything the app does, so the poorest-connected farmer is never left out.

04 — Product

How it works, start to sale.

A four-step flow a farmer can complete from a village with patchy signal.

01

List

The farmer enters crop, quantity, and location through the app, an SMS, or a call to the helpline.

02

Match

The system instantly matches them with nearby verified buyers, mandis, and cold storage.

03

Move

The farmer picks the best offer, and Kisan Setu helps arrange transport if it's needed.

04

Get Paid

Payment is tracked digitally, building a credit history the farmer can use for future loans.

Over time, the same data tells farmers what to plant next based on regional demand trends — reducing future oversupply and waste.
05 — Technology

As IT/CSE students, this is where we build our real edge.

App & Frontend

Android-first, low-end friendly

Flutter / React Native, light on data, with a Hindi and regional-language UI from day one.

Backend & APIs

Node.js / Django REST

PostgreSQL database, hosted on scalable cloud — AWS or GCP free-tier to start.

SMS & Voice Layer

No smartphone needed

Twilio / SMS gateway plus IVR calls so farmers without a smartphone can list crops and get alerts.

AI / ML Layer

Price & demand forecasting

A model trained on mandi price data, plus a matching algorithm that ranks the best buyer or storage option.

Maps & Logistics

Route planning

Google Maps API for buyer and cold-storage distance, and routing to the nearest option.

Data & Security

Trust by design

Encrypted farmer data, OTP-based login, and cloud backups — the trust layer adoption depends on.

06 — Revenue Model

How we earn money.

Revenue is a small percentage of real transactions, not ads or hype — so it scales naturally with usage.

Transaction commission2–4% commission from the buyer or mandi on every successful sale made through Kisan Setu.
Cold storage listing feeStorage owners pay a small monthly fee to list live availability.
Kisan Setu ProA paid tier for farmers with price alerts, crop-planning advice, and a credit-history report (~₹99/month).
Logistics partner feeA commission from the transport and trucking partners we route business to.
Govt. & NGO partnershipsGrants and subsidies for reducing post-harvest crop loss.
Data insights (anonymized)Sold to agri-input companies and FPOs to help them plan demand and supply.
07 — Building the Team

Lean now, specialized later.

Founding — Year 1

Small & Lean

2–3 co-founders covering tech, operations, and farmer outreach. Keep it cheap and hands-on — talk to farmers directly first.

Ground Team

Village-Level Agents

Local youths trained as "Kisan Mitras" who help farmers list crops and build trust, paid on commission so cost scales with usage.

Growth Phase

Specialists

App developers, a logistics coordinator, and a data analyst — hired once revenue supports it, not before.

We recruit through agriculture colleges and FPOs — they already have the farmers' trust, which is the hardest thing for a new startup to earn.

09 — Roadmap

Path to a profitable business.

Phase 1

Prove It

  • Launch in 2–3 villages/blocks
  • Free to use, build trust
  • Manually match buyers via WhatsApp/calls
Phase 2

Monetize

  • Introduce commission on sales
  • Onboard cold storages as paid partners
  • Launch Kisan Setu Pro subscription
Phase 3

Scale

  • Expand district by district
  • Partner with state agri departments
  • Sell aggregated market-demand data

Because revenue is a small percentage of real transactions, the model scales naturally with usage — more farmers using it means more commission, more subscriptions, more data value.
